What the ECRU Exchange is: A registry and catalogue. It records, lists, and catalogues verified tCO₂e functional units by category (iron ECRU, cement ECRU, fertiliser ECRU, hydrogen ECRU, etc.). It issues unique identifiers, tracks provenance, prevents double-counting, and provides the verified data layer that makes the instruments credible. It is infrastructure — not a financial institution, not a trading venue, not a fund manager. It does not hold, manage, or trade financial instruments. The banking layer and fund management sit separately — see Finance Layer below.
